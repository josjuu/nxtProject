The exercises in this project are the following:
Driving a figure. This figure is either a triagle to an hexagone. It can also drive it clockwise and counter-clockwise.
Drive a meter. 
Escaping from a square where each side has a unique color and the only way to escape the square is driving over the given color.
Driving through a maze. This will continue until it is cornered.
Following a line of a given color.
